Pizza Yummy

Steps to make a pizza

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Prepare the pizza dough: mix flour, yeast, water, salt, and a bit of olive oil. Knead until smooth.
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Add your favorite toppings: cheese, veggies, meats, or herbs.
  6. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for 2–3 minutes.
  8. Slice the pizza, serve, and enjoy!

Ingrediants required

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (7g) active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1–2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional toppings: pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, olives, mushrooms, fresh basil
